Hit 'Space' to pause and continue. Click and drag the gears and their centers to change them. Click and drag horizontally on the text boxes to edit their variables. Hit 'X' or 'C' to clear the drawing ; 'H' hides the interface ; Hit 'r' to stop and rewind your piece with the mosue, vertically is more sensitive, hit 'r' again to continue.

This is a digital implementation of the Cycloid Drawing Machine. The machines is absolute genious and this is not a substitute, still I really wanted to explore the generative patterns and process behind it. The advantage here is you have unlimited gear wheels and no limitations for their axes & origins.
